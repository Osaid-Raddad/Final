Smashed Burger

Smashed Burger is a popular burger spot in Ramallah, Palestine, known for its quality and delicious offerings. It provides a variety of burger options, including smashed-style burgers which have gained quite a fan base. Located in a lively part of the city, Smashed Burger is a go-to place for both locals and visitors looking for a satisfying burger experience.
